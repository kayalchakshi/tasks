Welcome to Swiss Bakery !

Welcome to The Swiss Bakery.We are committed to very high quality and carrying on the culinary traditions
of the Swiss. Our bakery items are prepared fresh daily by our skilled staff in our Springfield, Virginia store.
In addition to producing fresh breads, cakes and pastries, our team of chefs has selected a discriminating
collection of imported gourmet foods, including many hard to find world class chocolates, coffees, teas,
and condiments.

Cakes for all Occasions

From handmade cookies to extravagant wedding cakes and everything in between, we make it right here in the shop. We have ready-made items and we're happy to create special orders.

Baking Tips

Use a good recipe. Use the tin size stated in the recipe and line it well, Preheat the oven, Be accurate with weights and ingredients, Get as much air into the cake as you can, Once the cake mixture is made put it straight into the oven, Stick to cooking times then Cooling cakes.
